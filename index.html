Description of the event

Vivid Sydney is a city-wide festival that transforms Sydney into a glowing playground of creativity. Over 23 nights, streets, landmarks and the harbour come alive with large-scale light art, projections, immersive installations, live music, talks and delicious food experiences.

It celebrates imagination and innovation - where art meets technology in a friendly, exciting atmosphere. Every corner feels different: some spaces are calm and dreamy, others loud and energetic. Step outside your everyday routine and discover something new after dark.

Practical information

Entry
Many outdoor light displays are free; some concerts, talks and food events require tickets.
Tickets
Buy tickets online: Official website
Getting there
Fly to Sydney Airport (SYD). Use trains, buses, ferries, or light rail to reach Circular Quay, Darling Harbour and nearby areas.
Accommodation (optional)
Stay near the city centre or harbour for easy walking access. Book early for popular weekends.

Quick tips

  • Dress warm - evenings in May/June can be chilly.
  • Visit mid-week for smaller crowds.
  • Charge your phone for photos!
